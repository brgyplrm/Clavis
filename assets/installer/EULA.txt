1. Local-Only Storage
Clavis is designed as a local-first password manager. All passwords, metadata, keys, and settings are stored locally on your device in an encrypted SQLCipher SQLite database. We do not operate any cloud database, backend server, or synchronization service. You are solely responsible for managing and backing up your database file.

2. Zero Telemetry & Privacy
Your privacy is paramount. Clavis does not collect, log, transmit, or share any telemetry, system details, crash reports, analytics, or user activity logs. The application runs entirely isolated from third-party networks, with outbound internet connections strictly restricted to optional HIBP breach queries.

3. Zero-Knowledge Framework
Your master password is never stored anywhere on disk or transmitted. It is exclusively used to derive the database encryption key in memory using Argon2id. We have no means of resetting your password or recovering your data. If you forget your master password, your vault cannot be decrypted, and your data is permanently lost.
